ℹ️ About This Tool

About the PDF Reorder Pages Tool (English)

The PDF Reorder Pages tool by ApneSoftware lets you rearrange the pages of a PDF into any order you like — by dragging thumbnails, moving pages step by step, or jumping a page straight to a chosen position — all directly inside your web browser. When pages end up out of sequence after scanning, merging or exporting, you can put them right in seconds and download a clean, correctly ordered PDF, without ever uploading your file to a server.

Introduction

Pages fall out of order surprisingly often: a double-sided scan interleaves fronts and backs, a batch of files is merged in the wrong sequence, an appendix ends up before the chapter it belongs to, or a cover page lands in the middle. Fixing this by re-exporting from the original source is slow and sometimes impossible. This tool gives you a visual board of every page so you can simply drag them into the right order and see the result immediately.

Because everything runs locally in your browser, the tool is well suited to sensitive material such as contracts, medical records, financial statements, legal filings and academic papers, where the pages must be in exact sequence but the document should never leave your device. There is nothing to install, no account to create and no watermark added to the output; you reorder as many documents as you need, as often as you need, and the finished PDF keeps its original page content, quality and text intact — only the page order changes.

Main Features

  • Drag & drop reordering that works with both a mouse and touch, so you can rearrange pages on a phone or tablet just as easily as on a desktop.
  • Precise moves: move a page up, down, to the first or last position, or type a target position to jump it there.
  • Multi-select with checkboxes to move several pages together, plus select all, none and invert.
  • Reverse the entire order in one click, or restore the original order at any time.
  • Undo, redo and reset so every change is safe and reversible.
  • Fullscreen preview with zoom, fit-width, fit-page and navigation to check a page before committing.
  • File-info panel showing name, size, page count and title, plus preserve-metadata and compress options.
  • Download or print the reordered PDF with a custom file name.

Benefits

The tool is fast, private and precise. Because you can see every page, you place each one exactly where it belongs; because pages are copied rather than re-encoded, their content and formatting are preserved with no quality loss; and because everything runs locally, there is no upload wait and no privacy risk. It is completely free, adds no watermark, needs no account, and places no limit on the number of pages or files.

Why Use This Tool

Many online reorder tools upload your PDF to a remote server, where a copy could be logged or cached. This one processes everything on your device, so confidential contracts, financial statements and identity documents never leave it. It also offers more than a basic mover — true drag & drop with touch support, multi-select group moves, jump-to-position, reverse, restore, undo/redo and a full-screen preview — in a clean, modern interface that works anywhere.

Common Use Cases

  • Fixing interleaved pages after a double-sided scan.
  • Reordering files that were merged in the wrong sequence.
  • Moving a cover page or appendix to the correct position.
  • Reversing a document that was scanned back-to-front.
  • Grouping related pages together before sharing or printing.

Privacy & Security

Privacy is built in. Your PDF is read with the browser's File API, previewed with a client-side PDF engine and rewritten with a client-side PDF library; nothing is transmitted, cached on a server or kept after you close the tab, and temporary data is released from memory when you reset. This makes the tool suitable for personal, business, legal, financial and medical documents that should never pass through a third party.

Supported Formats & Browser Compatibility

The tool works with standard PDF files and outputs a standard PDF. It runs in the latest Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari, Opera and Brave on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android and iOS. A modern browser with JavaScript is all you need — nothing to install.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Drag by the grip handle in the corner for the smoothest reordering.
  • Select several pages first to move a whole section in one go.
  • Use “Jump to position” for long documents where dragging far is tedious.
  • Open a page full-screen if you are unsure where it belongs.
  • Use Restore original if you want to start over without re-uploading.

Performance, Accessibility & Limitations

Pages are rendered once as compact thumbnails and reused as you rearrange them, so moving pages is instant and memory use stays low even for long documents; full-resolution rendering only happens in the preview. Every control is keyboard-reachable with clear labels, visible focus outlines and strong contrast in both themes, and there are shortcuts for undo, redo and save. As for limits, reordering changes page order only — it does not rotate, crop or delete pages (use the dedicated tools for those) — and complex bookmarks that point to specific pages may not follow a reorder, so check any table of contents afterwards.

Conclusion

PDF Reorder Pages turns a frustrating out-of-sequence document into a quick drag-and-drop fix — free, private and entirely in the browser. Whether you are correcting one misplaced page or reorganising a whole report, it gives you fast, lossless control over page order without your file ever leaving your device.
