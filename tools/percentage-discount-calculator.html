📊 Percentage & Discount Calculator

Four common percentage calculations in one place.

ℹ️ About This Tool

Percentages show up constantly in everyday life — discounts at a store, marks in an exam, interest on savings, year-over-year growth in a business report — yet the actual arithmetic behind a percentage question changes depending on which way you're solving it, which is exactly why a single calculator that handles all the common variations is so useful. This tool bundles together four of the most frequently needed percentage calculations: finding what a percentage of a number is, finding what percentage one number is of another, calculating the percentage increase or decrease between two values, and working out a discounted price — switching between them takes a single tap.

The first mode, "X% of Y", answers questions like figuring out a 20% tip on a restaurant bill or how much commission you'd earn on a sale. The second mode, "X is what % of Y", flips that around, which is what you need when you know two raw numbers and want to express their relationship as a percentage — for instance, figuring out what percentage of your monthly income went toward rent, or what fraction of a class scored above a certain mark.

The third mode handles percentage change, the calculation people get wrong most often because the formula isn't simply the difference between two numbers — it's the difference divided by the original value, expressed as a percentage. This is essential for understanding whether a price increase, a salary hike, a population change, or a business metric actually represents a big or small shift relative to where it started, since the same absolute change can be a huge percentage move on a small base or a tiny one on a large base.

The fourth mode is a dedicated discount calculator: enter the original price and the discount percentage, and it instantly shows you how much you save and what the final price will be — handy while shopping, comparing sale offers, or working out margins if you run a small business. Every calculation updates live as you type, with no separate "calculate" button to click, and since everything runs locally in your browser, you can use it as often as you like for quick mental-math backup without any data ever being sent anywhere. Whether you're a student checking exam scores, a shopper comparing two different sale percentages to see which actually saves more, or a small business owner working out markups and margins, having all four directions of percentage math in one place saves you from switching between separate calculators or re-deriving the formula each time.

How to use:

  1. Pick the calculation type from the tabs.
  2. Enter the values.
  3. The result updates instantly.
