ℹ️ About the Keyword Rank Tracker

A keyword rank tracker tells you exactly where your website appears in Google's search results for the words your customers actually type. Instead of scrolling through pages of Google yourself in a private window, you enter your domain and your keywords and get back the precise position, the exact page that ranks, and its URL — for one keyword or hundreds at once.

The rankings here come from live Google results, not guesses or cached data, and you can target a specific country and language so the numbers reflect the audience you care about rather than your own personalized results. Upload a CSV of keywords to check a whole campaign in one run, watch the progress fill in row by row, and export everything back to CSV for your reports or to compare against last week.

Tracking rank matters because SEO is a moving target: competitors publish, Google updates, and a page that sat at position 3 can quietly slip to 12. Checking regularly shows whether your content and link-building are working, which keywords are close to page one and worth a push, and which pages are winning so you can double down. It turns "I think we're doing okay" into concrete numbers you can act on.

Because the Google query runs on the server side, your searches are neutral and non-personalized, and your API key stays private. Everything else — building the keyword list, reading the table, exporting CSV — happens in your browser.

✨ Features

  • Real Google ranking position (top 100) with the exact ranking URL and page number.
  • Single keyword check or bulk CSV upload (up to 50 per run).
  • Country and language targeting for accurate local rankings.
  • Progress bar, summary stats, and one-click CSV export.

📝 How to use

  1. Enter your domain (for example example.com).
  2. Type keywords one per line, or upload a CSV.
  3. Pick the Google country and language.
  4. Click Check Rankings, then export the results.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these real Google rankings?

Yes — positions come from live Google search results via a SERP data provider, targeted by the country and language you choose.

Why is my rank different from what I see in my browser?

Google personalizes results by your location, device and history. This tool returns neutral, non-personalized rankings for the selected country/language.

How many keywords can I check at once?

Up to 50 per run. Enter them one per line or upload a CSV, and results appear as each keyword is checked.

What does "Not in top 100" mean?

Your domain did not appear anywhere in the first 100 Google results for that keyword — a sign there's ranking work to do.

Is my data private?

Your keyword list and results stay in your browser. Only the search query runs server-side so the API key stays hidden.

Is it free?

The tool is free; it uses a SERP provider whose free quota covers regular checking. Heavy bulk use may need a paid plan.
