ℹ️ About This Tool

The ApneSoftware Invoice & Billing Generator is a professional, privacy-first tool for creating clean, compliant invoices and billing documents directly in your browser — no accounting software, no account and no upload. For a freelancer, a shop, a clinic or a small business, invoicing is a daily reality, and the usual options are frustrating: spreadsheets that break their own formatting, paid software with monthly fees and per-invoice limits, or online generators that require a login and quietly store your clients' financial details on their servers. This tool removes all of that. You fill in a guided form, a live preview assembles a polished invoice as you type, the totals and taxes calculate themselves, and you export in whatever format you need — while every figure stays on your own device.

It is built for real billing, including India's GST requirements. You can produce a tax invoice, a plain invoice, a proforma invoice, a quotation or a receipt simply by choosing the document type, which sets the correct heading. Your business details include name, address, GSTIN, PAN, email and a logo; your client details include name, billing and shipping addresses and their GSTIN. Each line item carries a description, an HSN/SAC code, quantity, unit price and a per-item discount, and the tool sums it all into a subtotal automatically. You then choose a tax mode — no tax, a single rate, split CGST + SGST for intra-state sales, or IGST for inter-state — and the correct tax lines appear in the breakdown.

The calculations are the heart of the tool and they are completely automatic. As you edit items, discounts, shipping and additional charges, the preview recomputes the subtotal, the discount, the tax (as one figure or split into CGST/SGST/IGST), the shipping, an optional round-off, and the grand total in real time. The grand total is also written out as amount in words in the Indian numbering system — "One Lakh Twenty-Three Thousand…" — exactly as a compliant invoice requires, and the amount paid and amount due are tracked so partial and advance payments are clear. There is no risk of a manual arithmetic slip, and no formula to maintain.

Getting paid is made easy. Enter a UPI ID and the invoice shows a scannable UPI payment QR so a client can pay from any UPI app in seconds; add your bank name, account number and IFSC for transfers; and set the payment status — paid, unpaid, partial or advance — with a method such as UPI, bank transfer, cash, card or cheque. You can also add a logo and a signature image for a finished, official look, along with notes and terms and conditions — payment terms, return or warranty policy, or anything else — that print at the bottom. Three visual templates (modern, corporate and minimal) and an accent colour let you match your brand, and switching templates never loses your data.

Export and saving are flexible. Download a print-ready PDF to email to a client, a PNG image, a standalone HTML file, an editable Word (.doc) version, or a JSON file that captures the entire invoice as data. That JSON is also how you save and reload a draft: import it later to continue or to reuse as a template for the next invoice to the same client, or keep it as a record. On top of that the tool auto-saves your current invoice to the browser, so if you close the tab your work is still there when you return. There is no account to create, and validation checks your GSTIN, email and phone formats so mistakes are caught before you send.

Speed matters when billing is a chore you repeat. Because the preview is rendered from the same data on every keystroke, there is no separate "generate" step and no surprise at export time — the figures on screen are the figures in the PDF. That immediacy makes it quick to reuse: keep a JSON of last month's invoice for a regular client, import it, change the invoice number and dates, and you have this month's bill in under a minute. Switching the document type, template or currency re-flows the same content instantly, and the running totals box shows the grand total and amount due as you edit, so you always know exactly what you are charging before you send anything.

The tool is designed to fit many kinds of business. A freelancer can raise a simple service invoice with a UPI QR; a shop can itemise goods with HSN codes and split GST; a clinic or hotel can bill a mix of services with per-item discounts; and an exporter can issue a proforma invoice in another currency. Because every field is optional beyond the essentials, the same tool produces a minimal one-item receipt or a detailed multi-line tax invoice with equal ease, and the structured JSON means your billing history is portable and never locked inside a subscription you have to keep paying for.

Privacy is fundamental. Unlike many online invoice makers that require a login and upload your and your clients' financial information — names, addresses, GST numbers, amounts — to their servers, this tool does everything locally. Your invoice is assembled in the browser, saved only in your own browser storage, and exported straight to your device; nothing is transmitted, there is no account, and nothing about your billing is tracked or sold. For documents full of commercial and personal data, that matters, and it means the tool keeps working offline once loaded. The interface follows modern accessibility standards with keyboard-operable controls, ARIA labelling, visible focus outlines and high-contrast support. Importantly, this is a genuine, deterministic billing tool — there are no AI features and nothing is generated or sent to a model; you enter the details, and the tool calculates, formats and exports exactly what you typed. In short, it is a fast, capable and genuinely private invoicing studio that lives entirely in your browser — powerful enough for GST-compliant business billing, simple enough for a one-off freelance invoice.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🛠️ Troubleshooting

  • Tax isn't calculating: set the Tax mode to Single, CGST/SGST or IGST and enter a rate.
  • UPI QR not showing: enter a valid UPI ID (name@bank); the QR appears in the preview.
  • GSTIN shows a warning: a GSTIN is 15 characters; check the format or leave it blank.
  • PDF spans two pages: reduce the number of items or shorten notes to fit one page.
  • Lost my invoice: autosave keeps it on this device; export JSON as a backup.
  • Amount in words looks off: it uses the Indian format for INR; check the currency.
  • Nothing happens on export: allow downloads for the site and try another format.
