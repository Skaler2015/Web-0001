ℹ️ About PDF Compression

A PDF that's grown too large to email, too slow to upload to a portal, or simply takes up more storage than it should is one of the most common frustrations with the format — especially when the file is full of high-resolution scanned images or embedded photos. This tool shrinks PDF file size by re-encoding embedded images at a lower, still-readable quality and optimizing the document's internal structure, often cutting file size dramatically without making the text or images noticeably worse to the eye.

Unlike a single quality slider, this compressor analyzes your document first and gives you professional compression modes (Basic, Recommended, Strong, Maximum, Lossless and Custom), the ability to compress to an exact target size, and a one-click Smart Optimize that auto-selects the best balance. Lossless mode optimizes the file structure and keeps your text fully selectable, while the image modes are ideal for scanned and photo-heavy documents.

✅ Benefits

  • Get under email and portal upload limits (often 20–25 MB) in seconds.
  • Save cloud storage when archiving years of scanned documents.
  • Stay private — files never leave your device, safe for confidential papers.
  • Batch-compress many PDFs and download them together as a ZIP.

⚙️ How It Works

For image modes, each page is rendered to a canvas at a chosen DPI and re-encoded as a JPEG at a target quality, then assembled into a new, smaller PDF. For Lossless mode, the document is re-saved with optimized object streams and (optionally) stripped metadata, with no quality loss. The target-size feature probes the first page at several quality levels to model the size curve, then picks the highest quality that still fits your target.

🧩 Compression Methods

  • Basic — light touch, near-original quality.
  • Recommended — best balance of size and quality.
  • Strong / Maximum — prioritize the smallest file.
  • Lossless — structure optimization, keeps selectable text.
  • Custom & Target Size — full control or an exact size goal.

🔒 Privacy

All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript and open PDF libraries. Nothing is uploaded, the tool works offline once loaded, and your file is released from memory when you close or reset the page.

❓ FAQ

Can I compress to an exact size? Yes — pick a target like 1 MB and the tool finds the best level automatically.

Will my text stay selectable? In Lossless mode, yes. Image modes convert pages to images for maximum reduction.

Is it free and private? Completely free, no signup, and 100% in-browser.
