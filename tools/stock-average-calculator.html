ℹ️ About the Stock Average Calculator

Averaging is one of the most common things an investor does: you buy a share at one price, it falls, and you buy more to bring your average buy price down. This free Stock Average Calculator does that maths instantly and goes much further — letting you add unlimited buy entries (or upload a CSV of your trades), plan a target average, work out sell profit/loss, estimate SIP returns, real brokerage & charges, capital-gains tax, position size for risk management, and adjust for bonus & splits.

✨ What you can do

  • Average price across any number of buys, with a live chart.
  • Target average — how many extra shares to buy to reach a chosen average.
  • Sell P&L, SIP & lumpsum future value, brokerage, tax.
  • Risk & position size, bonus/split & dividend, scenario simulator, portfolio allocation.
  • CSV upload and PDF export of your summary.

📝 How to use

  1. On the Average tab, add the quantity and price of every purchase.
  2. Read your average buy price, total quantity and total investment instantly.
  3. Switch tabs to plan a target average, sell P&L, SIP, charges, tax and more.
  4. Export a PDF summary or copy the result.
