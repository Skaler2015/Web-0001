ℹ️ About This Barcode Generator Tool

Barcodes are machine-readable patterns of parallel lines that encode data in a format scanners can read instantly. They are used everywhere — from supermarket product labels to warehouse inventory systems, shipping labels, hospital patient wristbands, library books, airline boarding passes, and government identification documents. This free online barcode generator supports all major 1D barcode formats plus QR codes, allowing you to create professional scannable barcodes without any software installation or registration.

The tool supports CODE128 — the most versatile and widely used 1D barcode format that can encode any ASCII character including letters, numbers, and symbols, making it ideal for inventory codes, serial numbers, and shipping labels. EAN-13 is the international standard for retail product barcodes used on most consumer goods sold globally. UPC-A (Universal Product Code) is the North American retail standard. EAN-8 is a shorter version for small products. CODE39 is used in automotive, defense, and healthcare. ITF-14 is used for shipping cartons. Pharmacode is used in the pharmaceutical industry.

Beyond basic generation, this tool offers professional customization: choose barcode and background colors, adjust bar width and height, control margins, select font size and family, toggle bold text, and place text above or below the barcode. Download in PNG (for web and documents), SVG (scalable vector, ideal for print at any resolution), or PDF (print-ready). The batch generator lets you upload a CSV file with hundreds of values and download all barcodes at once as a ZIP file — saving significant time in inventory and logistics workflows.

The QR Code generator creates 2D barcodes that can store URLs, contact information, WiFi credentials, plain text, and more. QR codes are scannable by any modern smartphone camera without a dedicated app, making them ideal for marketing materials, product packaging, menus, and contactless information sharing. You can customize the QR color, background color, size, and error correction level.

All barcode generation happens entirely in your browser — no data is ever sent to any server. Your barcode values and history are private. The recent barcodes history feature saves your last 20 generated barcodes in LocalStorage for quick regeneration.

📖 How to Use:

  1. Enter the text or number you want to encode in the input field.
  2. Select the barcode format appropriate for your use case (CODE128 works for most purposes).
  3. Adjust size, colors, font, and margin using the controls.
  4. Click PNG, SVG, or PDF to download. Use Print for instant printing.
  5. For QR codes, switch to the QR Code tab and enter your URL or text.
  6. For multiple barcodes, use the Batch tab — upload a CSV or paste values.

📦 Supported Barcode Formats:

  • CODE128: Any ASCII text. Most versatile. Logistics, inventory, general use.
  • CODE39: Alphanumeric (A-Z, 0-9). Automotive, defense, healthcare.
  • EAN-13: Exactly 13 digits. Retail products worldwide.
  • EAN-8: Exactly 8 digits. Small retail products.
  • UPC-A: Exactly 12 digits. North American retail products.
  • ITF-14: 14 digits. Shipping cartons and outer packaging.
  • MSI: Numeric only. Retail shelf labels, inventory.
  • Pharmacode: Numeric 3–131071. Pharmaceutical packaging.
  • Codabar: Numeric + special chars. Blood banks, libraries.
  • QR Code: 2D. URLs, text, contact info, WiFi. Smartphone scannable.
