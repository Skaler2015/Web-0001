ℹ️ About this tool

The ID Card Crop & Print tool is built for anyone who takes a quick phone photo of an identity card and needs a clean, correctly sized printout — print shops, cyber cafés, offices, schools and everyday users. Instead of manually cropping the jeans, table or hand out of the photo in a photo editor, you simply drop the front and back pictures here. The tool finds the card, removes everything around it, straightens any tilt, and lays both sides out on a single sheet at the real credit-card size (CR80, 85.6 × 54 mm) so the print looks exactly like the original card.

Because the entire process runs inside your browser using JavaScript and the HTML canvas, your images are never uploaded to any server. This matters a great deal for identity documents such as Aadhaar, PAN and driving licences, which contain sensitive personal data. Nothing is stored, logged or transmitted — close the tab and every trace is gone.
