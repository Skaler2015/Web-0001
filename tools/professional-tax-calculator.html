01. Introduction

Professional Tax (PT) is a state-levied tax on salaried employees and self-employed professionals in India, deducted directly from salary or paid by registration, and capped constitutionally at ₹2,500 per year — yet the actual slab, whether it's charged monthly, half-yearly, or annually, and whether it applies at all, varies considerably from state to state. The Professional Tax Calculator automatically loads the correct state-wise slab, computes Employee, Employer and Self-Employed liability, checks common exemptions, and compares two states side by side — instantly, in your browser, with nothing ever uploaded.

02. State-wise Professional Tax Slab Engine

Twelve states with well-documented current slabs are built in — Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Kerala, Assam, Bihar, Odisha and Madhya Pradesh — each modelled with its actual billing cadence (most states monthly, Tamil Nadu and Kerala half-yearly, Bihar annually based on total annual income) and, where applicable, the higher February instalment several states use to reach the exact ₹2,500 annual cap. States where PT genuinely doesn't apply (Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, Haryana) show that clearly, and states this calculator doesn't yet have a verified current slab for (Tripura, Meghalaya, Sikkim, Puducherry) are disclosed honestly rather than showing a fabricated number.

03. Employee Information & Salary Details

Enter employee and employer names, state, employment type, category, gender, age, financial year and salary frequency, along with a full salary breakdown — Basic, HRA, DA, Conveyance, Medical, Special Allowance, Bonus, Incentives, Overtime and Other Earnings — which combine automatically into your Gross Salary.

04. Live Calculation Engine

Monthly PT, Annual PT, Salary After PT, Annual Salary, Monthly Deduction, Total Deduction and Effective Deduction % all update instantly as you type, with the exact slab and rule applied shown transparently.

05. Employer Mode

Add each employee's monthly salary to estimate total monthly and annual Professional Tax liability across your whole workforce for the selected state.

06. Self-Employed Professional Calculator

Registered self-employed professionals are estimated at the state's statutory annual cap as a reasonable approximation, since most states charge a flat annual enrolment amount for professionals rather than a salary-based slab.

07. Exemption Checker

Tick Senior Citizen, Armed Forces, Parent of a Disabled Child, Disabled Person, or a State-Specific Exemption, and the calculator flags Professional Tax as ₹0 with the specific reason and applicable rule.

08. Compare States

Select any two states to see Monthly PT, Annual PT, the exact difference, and each state's applicable rule side by side — useful when comparing job offers across states or planning a multi-state payroll.

09. Export & Share

10. Privacy

13. Frequently Asked Questions | अक्सर पूछे जाने वाले प्रश्न

What is Professional Tax?

A state-levied tax on salaried employees and professionals, capped at ₹2,500 per year.

Professional Tax क्या है?

वेतनभोगी कर्मचारियों और पेशेवरों पर राज्य द्वारा लगाया गया कर, ₹2,500 प्रति वर्ष तक सीमित।

Who pays Professional Tax?

Salaried employees (deducted by the employer) and self-employed professionals (paid directly).

Professional Tax कौन देता है?

वेतनभोगी कर्मचारी (employer द्वारा काटा गया) और स्व-रोज़गार पेशेवर (सीधे भुगतान)।

How is Professional Tax calculated?

By matching your monthly (or half-yearly/annual) salary against your state's published slab.

Professional Tax की गणना कैसे होती है?

आपकी मासिक (या half-yearly/annual) salary को आपके राज्य के प्रकाशित slab से मिलाकर।

Is Professional Tax applicable in every state?

No — states like Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan and Haryana do not levy it.

क्या Professional Tax हर राज्य में लागू है?

नहीं — Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan और Haryana जैसे राज्य इसे नहीं लगाते।

What is the maximum Professional Tax?

₹2,500 per year, a constitutional cap under Article 276.

अधिकतम Professional Tax क्या है?

₹2,500 प्रति वर्ष, Article 276 के तहत एक constitutional cap।

What is PT in a salary slip?

A line-item deduction representing the Professional Tax withheld for that pay period.

Salary slip में PT क्या है?

उस pay period के लिए काटा गया Professional Tax दिखाने वाला एक line-item deduction।

Can employers deduct Professional Tax?

Yes — employers are legally required to deduct and deposit it on the employee's behalf.

क्या employers Professional Tax काट सकते हैं?

हाँ — employers को यह काटकर employee की ओर से जमा करना कानूनी रूप से आवश्यक है।

Are self-employed professionals required to pay PT?

Yes, in states where PT applies, via direct registration and payment.

क्या स्व-रोज़गार पेशेवरों को PT देना ज़रूरी है?

हाँ, उन राज्यों में जहाँ PT लागू है, direct registration और भुगतान के माध्यम से।

What are Professional Tax exemptions?

Senior citizens, armed forces personnel, parents of disabled children, and disabled persons are commonly exempt.

Professional Tax छूट क्या हैं?

Senior citizens, armed forces personnel, disabled बच्चों के parents, और disabled व्यक्ति आमतौर पर exempt हैं।

Is Professional Tax deductible under Income Tax?

Yes — Professional Tax paid is deductible from gross salary under Section 16 of the Income Tax Act.

क्या Professional Tax Income Tax के तहत deductible है?

हाँ — भुगतान किया गया Professional Tax Income Tax Act की Section 16 के तहत gross salary से deductible है।

How often should Professional Tax be paid?

Depends on the state — most are monthly, some (Tamil Nadu, Kerala) half-yearly, and Bihar annually.

Professional Tax कितनी बार देना चाहिए?

राज्य पर निर्भर — अधिकतर मासिक, कुछ (Tamil Nadu, Kerala) half-yearly, और Bihar annually।

What happens if PT is not paid?

The employer or professional may face interest and penalty under the state's PT Act.

अगर PT नहीं दिया जाए तो क्या होता है?

Employer या professional को राज्य के PT Act के तहत interest और penalty का सामना करना पड़ सकता है।

Can PT rates change every year?

Yes — each state can revise its slab independently at any time.

क्या PT दरें हर साल बदल सकती हैं?

हाँ — प्रत्येक राज्य किसी भी समय स्वतंत्र रूप से अपना slab संशोधित कर सकता है।

Which states levy Professional Tax?

Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Kerala, Assam, Bihar, Odisha, Madhya Pradesh and several others.

कौन-से राज्य Professional Tax लगाते हैं?

Maharashtra, Karnataka, West Bengal, Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, Kerala, Assam, Bihar, Odisha, Madhya Pradesh और कई अन्य।

Why does Maharashtra charge more in February?

To reach the exact ₹2,500 annual cap, since 200×11+300=2,500.

Maharashtra फरवरी में ज़्यादा क्यों लेता है?

ठीक ₹2,500 वार्षिक cap तक पहुँचने के लिए, चूँकि 200×11+300=2,500।

Does gender affect the Professional Tax slab?

Yes, in Maharashtra — women have a higher nil-tax threshold (₹25,000) than men (₹7,500).

क्या gender Professional Tax slab को प्रभावित करता है?

हाँ, Maharashtra में — महिलाओं के लिए nil-tax threshold (₹25,000) पुरुषों (₹7,500) से अधिक है।

How does Tamil Nadu's half-yearly system work?

PT is assessed on your total half-yearly gross salary against a half-yearly slab, twice a year.

Tamil Nadu की half-yearly प्रणाली कैसे काम करती है?

PT का आकलन आपकी कुल half-yearly gross salary पर half-yearly slab के विरुद्ध, साल में दो बार होता है।

How does Bihar's system differ?

Bihar charges PT once a year based on your total annual income, not a monthly slab.

Bihar की प्रणाली कैसे अलग है?

Bihar साल में एक बार आपकी कुल annual income के आधार पर PT लगाता है, monthly slab नहीं।
