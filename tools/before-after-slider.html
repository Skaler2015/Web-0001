ℹ️ About This Tool

Compare two images interactively with a draggable slider — perfect for showing before/after edits, renovation results, weight loss progress, or any visual comparison.

How to use:

  1. Upload a "Before" image and an "After" image.
  2. Drag the slider handle left and right to reveal each image.
