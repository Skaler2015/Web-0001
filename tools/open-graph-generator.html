📘 What are Open Graph Tags?

Open Graph (OG) tags are meta tags developed by Facebook that control how your webpage appears when shared on social media platforms including Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, Discord and many others. They let you define exactly which title, description, and image is shown — rather than letting each platform guess.

Without OG tags, social platforms crawl your page and pick whatever content they find — often the wrong image, a truncated title, or no description at all. With proper OG tags, every share of your link looks professional, branded and enticing, which directly increases click-through rates from social media.

The Essential OG Tags

  1. og:title — The headline shown in the shared post
  2. og:description — The summary text below the title
  3. og:image — The preview image (1200×630px recommended)
  4. og:url — The canonical URL of the page
  5. og:type — Content type (website, article, product…)
  6. og:site_name — Your brand name
  7. og:locale — Language and region

कौन से OG Tags ज़रूरी हैं?

  1. og:title — शेयर होने पर दिखने वाला शीर्षक
  2. og:description — शीर्षक के नीचे का विवरण
  3. og:image — प्रीव्यू इमेज (1200×630px)
  4. og:url — पेज का canonical URL
  5. og:type — कंटेंट टाइप (website, article…)
  6. og:site_name — आपका ब्रांड नाम

Image के लिए Best Practices

  1. Image size: 1200×630 pixels (Facebook recommended)
  2. File size: 8 MB से कम
  3. Format: JPG या PNG
  4. URL: हमेशा https:// से शुरू होना चाहिए
  5. Text in image: 20% से कम रखें
  6. Image URL: Absolute URL होनी चाहिए, relative नहीं
