    ℹ️ About This Tool — Advanced Word Counter

    The ApneSoftware Word Counter is a free, advanced online tool that instantly counts the words, characters, sentences, lines and paragraphs in your text as you type or paste it. But it is far more than a simple counter: it is a complete writing dashboard that also measures reading time, speaking time, readability, keyword density, vocabulary diversity and much more — all running entirely inside your web browser, with nothing ever uploaded to a server.

    Whether you are a student meeting an essay word limit, a blogger aiming for the ideal post length, an SEO professional fitting a title and meta description within Google's limits, a copywriter trimming an advertisement, a novelist tracking a daily goal, or a social-media manager keeping a caption under a platform's cap, this tool gives you every number you need to write with confidence and precision.

    Everything it measures

    Alongside the core word and character counts, the tool shows characters with and without spaces, sentence and paragraph counts, an estimated page count, and both reading and speaking time. Its detailed statistics panel breaks your text down further — average words per sentence, average word length, longest and shortest words, and counts of emojis, URLs, emails, hashtags and mentions — so you understand not just how much you have written, but how it is structured.

    Readability and writing analysis

    The readability panel calculates the Flesch Reading Ease score and the Flesch–Kincaid grade level, telling you at a glance whether your writing suits your audience. The writing analysis flags long sentences and likely passive-voice phrases, and the AI style scores offer clear, rule-based suggestions to make your text tighter and easier to read. Watching these numbers change as you edit is one of the fastest ways to learn what clear writing looks like.

    Keyword density and SEO

    For anyone writing for the web, the keyword analysis lists your most-used words with their density and a bar chart, while ignoring common stop words. The social-media limit checker shows live progress against the character limits of Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, an SMS, a Google title and a meta description, so your content always fits where it is published.

    Productivity and export

    Writing tools include a text cleaner (remove extra spaces, blank lines, duplicate lines, HTML, numbers, emojis and more), a case converter, find and replace with regular-expression support, undo and redo, automatic saving in your browser, a distraction-free focus mode and a fullscreen mode. When you are finished you can export your text or report as TXT, PDF, DOC or CSV, print it, or copy the statistics, keywords or full report with a single click.

    Private, offline and free

    Because every calculation happens locally in your browser using JavaScript, your text never leaves your device, is never uploaded and is never stored on any server. This makes the tool completely private — safe for confidential documents — and means it keeps working even without an internet connection once the page has loaded. There is no signup, no watermark and no limit; it is entirely free to use as often as you like.

    Who it is for

    Students rely on it to hit exact assignment limits without losing their flow, and teachers use it to check submissions. Bloggers and content writers use the length, reading-time and readability figures to plan posts that both rank well and keep readers engaged. SEO specialists check title and description lengths and keyword balance. Authors and screenwriters track daily word-count goals over long projects, helped by the auto-save and focus mode. Translators and freelancers price and plan work by word count, and social-media managers craft captions that fit every platform. In short, anyone who writes anything with a length, a limit or an audience attached will find it useful.

    Why choose this Word Counter

    Many online counters send your text to a server, show only a single number, or hide useful features behind a paywall. This one does the opposite: it keeps your text private, gives you a full professional writing dashboard, and is completely free with no account required. It is fast, accurate, mobile-friendly and works offline, combining the instant counting of a word processor with analytics that help you write measurably better. Keep it open in a tab whenever you write, and let the numbers guide you toward clearer, better-fitted and more effective writing every time.

    How to use

    1. Type or paste your text into the box above.
    2. Watch every count and statistic update live as you write.
    3. Open the readability, keyword, social-media and target panels to refine your writing.
    4. Clean, convert or find-and-replace your text as needed.
    5. Export, print or copy your finished text and report.
