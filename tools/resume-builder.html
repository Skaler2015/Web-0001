ℹ️ About This Tool

Build a clean, professional one-page resume by filling out a simple form — no design skills needed. Add as many work experience and education entries as you need, and watch the preview update instantly before downloading as a PDF.

How to use:

  1. Fill in your personal details, summary, experience, education, and skills.
  2. Use "+ Add Experience" or "+ Add Education" for multiple entries.
  3. Pick an accent color, then click Download PDF.
