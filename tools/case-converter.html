ℹ️ About Case Converter

The ApneSoftware Case Converter is a free, fast and privacy-friendly online tool that transforms your text into any letter case you need. Whether you are writing a heading, formatting code, or cleaning up messy text, it does the job instantly — right in your browser, with no signup and nothing uploaded to a server.

✨ Features

  • 18+ case formats: UPPERCASE, lowercase, Title Case, Sentence case, aLtErNaTe, Toggle, Inverse, Capitalize Words, camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, kebab-case, CONSTANT_CASE, dot.case, path/case, Header-Case and Train-Case.
  • Text cleaning: remove extra spaces, empty/duplicate lines, tabs, special characters, numbers, letters, emojis and HTML tags.
  • Text utilities: reverse text/words/lines, shuffle & sort lines, number lines, duplicate lines, merge and split by delimiter.
  • Live statistics: characters, words, sentences, paragraphs, lines plus reading, speaking and typing time.
  • Export: copy, download as TXT, PDF or DOC, upload & drag-and-drop files, dark/light theme and keyboard shortcuts.

📝 How to Use

  1. Type, paste, upload or drag-and-drop your text into the input box.
  2. Click any format — for example UPPERCASE, Title Case or snake_case.
  3. Use the cleaning and utility buttons to refine the text further.
  4. Copy the result or download it as TXT, PDF or DOC.
  5. Turn on Live / Auto Convert to apply your last choice automatically as you type.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free? Yes — completely free with no signup.

Is my text safe? Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser; your text is never uploaded.

Does it support developer cases? Yes — camelCase, PascalCase, snake_case, kebab-case, CONSTANT_CASE and more.

Does it work on mobile? Yes, the layout is fully responsive with light and dark themes.
