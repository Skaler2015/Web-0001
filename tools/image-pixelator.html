Draw a box on the image below (drag with mouse or finger) to pixelate that area. Draw multiple times for multiple areas.

ℹ️ Everything happens locally in your browser — the photo is never uploaded anywhere.

ℹ️ About This Tool

Blur out faces, license plates, or other sensitive details in a photo by drawing a box over them — the area is replaced with a chunky pixelated effect, similar to TV censoring. Useful for privacy before posting photos online.

How to use:

  1. Upload a photo.
  2. Set the pixel block size, then drag a box over each area you want to hide.
  3. Click Download when you're done.
