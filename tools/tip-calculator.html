ℹ️ About This Tool

Splitting a restaurant bill with friends or family sounds like it should be simple arithmetic, but it's surprisingly easy to get wrong when you're doing it in your head at a noisy table after a meal — calculating a tip percentage, adding it to the bill, and then dividing the whole thing evenly across however many people are paying involves three separate steps, any one of which can go wrong, especially after a few drinks or when someone wants to split it a slightly different way. This tool handles the entire chain in one place so nobody has to pull out a phone calculator and start over twice.

You start by entering the bill amount, then pick a tip percentage from common presets like 5%, 10%, 15%, or 20%, or type in any custom percentage if your usual habit doesn't match those defaults — tipping norms vary a lot depending on where you are and the type of service, so having a custom option matters. Finally, enter how many people are splitting the bill, and the tool instantly shows the tip amount, the new total including tip, and exactly how much each person owes.

This is genuinely useful beyond just restaurants — it works just as well for splitting a cab fare with a service charge, dividing a group order for food delivery, or working out everyone's share of a hotel bill that includes a service fee. Because the per-person amount updates live as you change any input, it's easy to test a few different tip percentages on the spot if the group is debating how generous to be, or to quickly recalculate if someone leaves the table and the remaining people need to cover a slightly larger share.

The whole calculation happens instantly in your browser as you type, with no need to tap a separate "calculate" button, which matters when you're standing around a table waiting to settle up. There's nothing to install, no account needed, and no internet round-trip required once the page has loaded, so it works just as reliably on a spotty restaurant Wi-Fi connection as it does at home — open it, plug in the numbers, and you have an answer everyone at the table can agree on in seconds. It's a small tool, but it quietly removes one of the most common small frictions of eating out with a group — the slightly awkward few minutes of mental math and gentle disagreement that happens right when everyone's reaching for their wallets.

How to use:

  1. Enter the bill amount.
  2. Pick a tip percentage or enter a custom one.
  3. Enter how many people are splitting it — the per-person amount updates instantly.
