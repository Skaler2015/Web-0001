Financial Calculators

Our financial calculators — including the EMI Calculator, Income Tax Calculator, GST Calculator, SIP Calculator, HRA Calculator, Gratuity Calculator, and others — provide estimates based on standard formulas and publicly available tax information.

  • EMI calculations assume a standard reducing balance method and may vary slightly from your bank's calculation due to rounding or specific bank policies.
  • Income tax calculations are based on CBDT notifications and may not reflect the latest amendments. Always verify with the official income tax portal at incometaxindia.gov.in.
  • GST calculations are based on rates as notified by the GST Council. Rate changes may not be immediately reflected.

Please consult a qualified Chartered Accountant, financial advisor, or tax professional for all important financial decisions.
